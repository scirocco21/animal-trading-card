The European Hedgehog

The name for a baby hedgehog is "hoglet"...how cute is that?

  • Habitat: Grasslands, woodland and meadows
  • Diet: Mostly bettles, insects and earthworms)
  • Lifespan: Up to seven years
  • Size: Head and body, 13-30 cm
  • Weight: 400-1,000g (average 700g).

Hedgehogs get their name from their foraging habits: at night, they rummage through undergrowth and gardens in search of small critters. They hibernate in winter and need to be well fed to survive; hoglets born in late summer or autumn may appreciate a little help with fattening up.
